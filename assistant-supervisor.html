Assistant Supervisor

📌 Job Description

📍 Location: All TopTown Clothing retail stores

💰 Salary Range: K400 – K600 per fortnight

📆 Employment Type: Full-Time

⏳ Working Hours: Monday – Saturday (8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, compulsory on Fridays & Saturdays)

🔹 Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in meeting weekly and monthly sales targets.
  • Monitor staff attendance, punctuality, and discipline.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies (no phone use, no excessive conversations).
  • Maintain store cleanliness, organization, and stock arrangement.
  • Conduct inventory checks and ensure accurate stock records.
  • Handle customer complaints and inquiries professionally.
  • Submit daily and weekly reports on sales, staff performance, and stock.

✅ Work Conditions

  • Six (6) days per week, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
  • One (1) week written notice for resignation.
  • Termination due to policy violations or poor performance.

📅 Leave Entitlements

  • Annual Leave: As per PNG labor law.
  • Sick Leave: Requires a medical certificate.
  • Public Holidays: Compensatory day off if required to work.
  • Maternity Leave: Laid off but may reapply after 1 year.
