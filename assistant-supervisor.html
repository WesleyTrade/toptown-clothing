1. Position Summary

The Assistant Supervisor supports the Store Supervisor in overseeing daily store operations. This includes ensuring sales targets are met, staff performance is maintained, and store operations run smoothly.

2. Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in achieving weekly and monthly sales targets.
  • Monitor staff attendance, punctuality, and break times.
  • Train and guide new employees on store policies.
  • Ensure store cleanliness and inventory organization.
  • Enforce company policies, including no phone usage during work hours.
  • Prepare daily sales and staff performance reports.

3. Minimum Requirements

  • At least 1 year of experience as a Pricing Clerk or in a similar retail role.
  • Basic leadership & supervision experience.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet sales targets.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.
