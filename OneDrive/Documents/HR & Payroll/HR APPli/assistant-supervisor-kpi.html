Assistant Supervisor KPI Assessment Criteria

1. Sales & Revenue Performance (30%)

Target: Support the Supervisor in achieving weekly and monthly sales targets.

Assessment Criteria:

  • Contribution to sales performance.
  • Effectiveness in upselling & cross-selling.
  • Customer purchase volume & satisfaction feedback.

2. Staff Supervision & Discipline (20%)

Target: Ensure staff attendance, discipline, and compliance with company policies.

Assessment Criteria:

  • Monitoring staff punctuality and break times.
  • Enforcing company rules (e.g., no phones, no excessive conversations).
  • Handling staff misconduct & reporting issues to the Supervisor.

3. Store Operations & Inventory Management (20%)

Target: Maintain store cleanliness, stock organization, and efficiency.

Assessment Criteria:

  • Store cleanliness & weekly store audits.
  • Inventory management, stock checks & restocking.
  • Ensuring product placement & displays are correct.

4. Customer Service & Complaint Handling (15%)

Target: Ensure high customer service standards and resolve complaints.

Assessment Criteria:

  • Customer satisfaction ratings (feedback, surveys).
  • Number of customer complaints & resolution effectiveness.
  • Handling difficult customer interactions professionally.

5. Reporting & Communication (15%)

Target: Ensure accurate and timely submission of reports to the Supervisor.

Assessment Criteria:

  • Daily sales & performance reports submitted on time.
  • Accuracy of sales, attendance, and stock reports.
  • Effective communication with staff and management.
